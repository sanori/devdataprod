Motivation (2)

  • Graphical presentation makes data understand
  • You can notice Petal length and Sepal length are good features to classify iris species

  • Limitation: graph can show at most two variables. (except category)

Iris Data Explorer

  • https://sanori.shinyapps.io/irisApp
  • Explores iris data in various ways
  • You can choose x and y axis with any variables in data
  • Explorer shows the graph of two variables and species
  • It also shows the centers of each species if you want
